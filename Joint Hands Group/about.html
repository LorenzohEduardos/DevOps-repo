Company Story

Founded in 2018 in Migori County, Kenya, Joint Hands Group embarked on a mission to uplift local communities through the power of agroforestry. Our journey began with a clear vision: to foster environmental stewardship and create job opportunities that resonate with the heartbeat of our land. Our Roots: We planted our roots firmly in the fertile soil of Migori, nurturing seedlings in our nurseries with the care they deserve. By selling and planting these seedlings to smallholder farmers and organizations, we have not only greened the landscape but also sown the seeds of prosperity. Our Growth: As we grew, so did our ambitions. We expanded our tree planting initiatives, setting higher targets each year, and introduced innovative agroforestry models to enhance biodiversity. Our commitment to knowledge transfer led us to develop specialized training modules for farmers, ensuring the best farm practices are not just taught but implemented. Our Branches: Our branches reached out to embrace technology, integrating digital systems for record-keeping and exploring mobile applications for training. We invested in renewable energy solutions, all while keeping our operations grounded in sustainability. Our Impact: Today, Joint Hands Group stands as a testament to what can be achieved when hands join together for a common cause. We have become a knowledge-sharing hub, a champion of environmental impact assessments, and a beacon of sustainable practices.

Mission

To champion community empowerment and environmental stewardship through sustainable agroforestry, nurturing a symbiotic relationship between people and the planet

Vision

Envisioning a world where every community flourishes in harmony with nature, propelled by the principles of sustainable agriculture and responsible resource management.
