Joint Hands Group: Cultivating Growth and Sustainability

Welcome to Joint Hands Group, a pioneering organization dedicated to agroforestry andn community development. Since our founding in 2018, we've been at the forefront of empowering comunities by selling highg quality seedlings, training farmers and promoting sustainable agricultural practice

Our Initiaives

Tree Planting Expansion

We are scaling our tree planting efforts to new heights, setting ambitious annual goals, foreign partnerships with local enterprises, and rolling out cutting-edge agroforestry models to enrich biodiversity.

Enhanced Training Programs

Knowledge is power, and we are amplifying this by crafting specialized training modules, reaching out to various community segments, and ensuring the practical application of sustainable practices through vigilant monitoring.

Community Engagement

We believe in the strength of unity. By bolstering existing partnerships, hosting regular forums, and serving as a regional knowledge hub, we are nurturing a collaborative environment for growth.

Technological Advancement

Efficiency is key in our journey. We are integrating digital solutions for record-keeping, exploring mobile apps for on-the-go training, and investing in renewable energy to streamline our operations.

Environmental Stewardship

Our commitment to the planet is unwavering. Guided by thorough environmental impact assessments, we are crafting a sustainability blueprint that encompasses waste reduction, water conservation, and carbon offset initiatives.



Join us in our mission to create a greener, more sustainable future. Together, we can make a difference—one seedling at a time.
